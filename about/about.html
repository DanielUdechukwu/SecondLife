About Us

We are a DIY electronics shop dedicated to giving your devices, be it mobile, laptop or desktop a second life, hence our name. We fix everything ranging form your PC boards, phone boards, custom pc builds and the rest. We also offer live support via our online channels in case you need any assistance remotely. We give optimal services with employees dedicated to delivering the best experience. Our offices hours are 8 am to 7 pm, Mondays to Fridays, and 11 am to 5 pm on Saturdays. Feel free to contact us via our contact lines or chat with a representative via social media.
